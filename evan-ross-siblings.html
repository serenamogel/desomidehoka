A Quick Check At Evan Ross’s Early Days And Parent’s Separation

Evan Ross was born on August 26, 1988, in Greenwich, Connecticut, to Arne Næss Jr. (businessman and mountaineer) and Diana Ross (entertainer) and was raised alongside his older brother Ross Næss. His ethnicity is driven by his Norwegian descent father and African-American mother. He holds American nationality and is 35 years old as of 2024.

His parents divorced in 2000. According to Wikipedia, he has three older maternal half-sisters named Rhonda (biological father Berry Gordy) and Tracee and Chudney (biological father Robert Ellis Silberstein). Moving on, Christoffer, Katinka, and Leona are his three older paternal half-siblings from his father’s first marriage, and Nicklas and Louis – two younger paternal half-brothers from his father’s third marriage.

#2: Tracee Ellis Ross

Tracee Joy Silberstein, better known as Tracee Ellis Ross, was born to Diana and Robert Ellis Silberstein on October 29, 1972. She is an actress who graduated from Brown University and started working at the William Esper Acting Studio, where she showcased her stunning acting performance. Some of her popular projects are “Girlfriends,” where she worked from 2000 to 2008, and “Black-Ish,” on which she appeared for its eight-season run. Also, it is said that Tracee played a big role in directing both of them.

She has her makeup line named PATTERN as well. She has won six consecutive nominations for lead actress at the Primetime Emmys from 2016 for “Black-Ish” and once won the Golden Globe for Best Performance by an Actress in a Television Series – Musical or Comedy in 2017.

#3: Chudney Ross

Chudney Ross, also known as Chudney Lane Silberstein, is an author, producer, production manager, and bookshop owner. She took her first breath on November 4, 1975, to Diana and Robert and graduated from Georgetown University. She has worked on the reality show “Fame” and produced the TV program “Mesmerized.” Apart from that, she was the talent coordinator on 2002’s “Livin’ Large” and the author of “The Lone Bean,” a children’s book. She is married to Joshua Faulkner, with whom she has two daughters, Callaway and Everlee.

Ross Naess is the older brother of Evan. He was born to Arne and Diana on October 7, 1987, and his full name is Ross Arne Naess.
