Is Macaulay Culkin Married? The Home Alone Star Life and Career

 Posted on September 21, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  685 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Macaulay Culkin is a well-known American actor and musician who rose to prominence in the 1990s as a child star. He is most remembered for his performance as Kevin McCallister in the Home Alone film series, which catapulted him to the top of the list of the highest-paid and most successful child actors of all time.

Other films in which he has appeared include My Girl, The Good Son, Richie Rich, and Party Monster. He is also the founder and lead singer of the satirical rock band The Pizza Underground, as well as the publisher and CEO of Bunny Ears, a humorous pop culture website and podcast. Here are some personal and professional facts about Macaulay Culkin.

Macaulay CulkinMarriage and Relationships

Macaulay Culkin is presently engaged to actress Brenda Song, whom he met while filming Changeland in 2017. Dakota Song Culkin, the couple’s first child, was born in April 2021.

Culkin previously married Rachel Miner, an actress whom he met when they were both 15 years old. They eloped when they were 18 in 1998, but separated in 2000 and divorced in 2002.

From 2002 to 2010, Culkin dated actress Mila Kunis for eight years, although they never married. They announced their split in 2011, claiming it was mutual and amicable.

Macaulay Culkin Net Worth and Earnings

As of 2023, Macaulay Culkin’s net worth is expected to be over $18 million. His acting career, which he began when he was four years old, is his primary source of income. In 1988, he had his cinematic debut in Rocket Gibraltar, and in 1990, he had his breakthrough with Home Alone, which grossed over $476 million worldwide and won him a Golden Globe nomination.

In 1992, he returned to reprise his role in Home Alone 2: Lost in New York, which made over $358 million worldwide. He also appeared in films such as My Girl, The Good Son, The Nutcracker, The Pagemaster, and Richie Rich, making him one of the highest-paid child actors in history.

He received $8 million for Home Alone 2, $5 million for Getting Even with Dad, $8 million for Richie Rich, and $15 million for The Good Son, according to reports. Culkin took a break from performing in 1995, following his parents’ divorce and legal struggle over his inheritance.

In 2003, he returned to television with a cameo appearance on Will & Grace and a role in Party Monster, both of which got excellent reviews. Since then, he’s appeared in movies like Saved!, Sex and Breakfast, Adam Green’s Aladdin, and Changeland.

He has also provided vocals for Robot Chicken and Kings. His musical pursuits, such as his band The Pizza Underground, which mimicked Velvet Underground songs with pizza-themed lyrics, and his website and podcast Bunny Ears, which features comedic sketches, interviews, and articles, have also garnered him money.

Macaulay Culkin Age and Height

Macaulay Culkin was born in New York City on August 26, 1980, to Kit Culkin, a former stage actor, and Patricia Brentrup, a telephone operator. He is the third child in a family of seven, with four brothers and two sisters. Jennifer, his half-sister, passed away in 2000.

Bonnie Bedelia is his paternal aunt. He has Irish, German, Norwegian, and French ancestors. He was raised as a Roman Catholic and attended Yorkville’s St. Joseph’s School and the Professional Children’s School. He also attended the School of American Ballet.

Culkin will be 43 years old in 2023. He stands 5 feet, 7 inches (170 cm) tall and weighs 123 pounds (56 kilograms). His hair is brown, and his eyes are blue. He frequently wears glasses and has a beard. He has a snake tattoo on his left arm.
