Park Kyung Lim Biography

Age & Height

Actress Park Kyung Lim is 44 years old. Her date of birth is 30 March 1979. Her real height is 5 feet and 2 inches.

Education

Park Kyung Lim studies at the School of Film and Acting at New York Film Academy, US. She left her education to join the Korean showbiz industry as a host.

Park Kyung Lim Husband & Son

Park Jung Hoon is the husband of actress Park Kyung Lim. They got married in 2007. She is blessed with a son named Park Min Joon.

Park Kyung Lim in The Devil’s Plan

Park Kyung Lim is part of the Netflix Korean show The Devil’s Plan. She played all the games in a very secure way. She focuses on saving herself more rather than focusing on pulling other’s legs.

Jo In Sung and Park Kyung Lim

Actress Park Kyung Lim and actor Jo In Sung are best friends. They worked together at the start of their career and became friends. They are still very good friends and Jo In sung also praised her in one of his interviews.

Showbiz Career

Park Kyung Lim joined the showbiz industry in 2000 as host. She has various shows on many famous Korean TV channels. She also appeared in many TV dramas in supporting roles.
