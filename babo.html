Babo- Biography

Babo was born in Mexico on November 16, 1976. The rapper is best known as the lead singer for the Mexican hip-hop collective Cartel de Santa. In 2014, he released his 8th album, “Golpe Avisa,” with the group.

He is joined by Rowan Rabia in Cartel de Santa. He initially formed Cartel de Santa in 1997. Babo and his friends began by improvising rap songs, which led to a burgeoning interest in music as a viable career.
