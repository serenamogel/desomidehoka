YouTube, Social Media Influencer, Investments & Endorsements

YouTube

David Dobrik is the King of YouTube.

Dobrik’s vlog channel had 18.9 million followers and 8.2 billion views as of February 2021.

Additionally, David established his own YouTube channel, “David Dobrik,” in 2015, based on his popularity on Vine.

His videos on it are typically comedic vlogs based on real-life or semi-scripted settings, and they frequently involve The Vlog Squad.

In August 2016, he launched his second YouTube channel, “David Dobrik Too.”

Apparently, Dobrik earned a Diamond Play Button from YouTube in December 2018 to celebrate his YouTube channel’s 10 million followers.
