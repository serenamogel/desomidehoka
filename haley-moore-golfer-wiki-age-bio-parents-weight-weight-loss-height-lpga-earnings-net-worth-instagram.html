Haley Moore Biography – Haley Moore Wiki

Haley Moore is an American professional golfer who currently competes on the LPGA Tour. She previously played on the Symetra Tour and the Cactus Tour. Moore attended San Pasqual High School in Escondido, California, the United States where she was a member of the golf team. Moore graduated in 2016. As an amateur, she recorded several wins including the 2011 Omni Tucson National 13-18, the 2012 Las Vegas Junior Girls, the 2014 ClubCorp Mission Hills Desert Junior, the 2017 Allstate Sugar Bowl Intercollegiate, Golfweek Conference Challenge, and the 2018 Hawkeye El Tigre Invitational. She also played in the 2015 Junior Solheim Cup.

She attended the University of Arizona, where she played collegiate golf, while there she attained position 21 on the World Amateur Golf Rankings. After graduating from the University of Arizona, Moore turned professional in June 2019 and competed on the Cactus Tour where she won three times. In August 2019, she started contending to become a full-time member of the LPGA Tour by playing on the LPGA Tour Qualifying school. In November 2019, Moore finished T11 at LPGA Q-Series in Pinehurst, North Carolina to earn status for the 2020 LPGA Tour season.

Haley Moore Age

Born Haley Nicole Moore was born on November 24, 1998, in Crystal Lake, Illinois, United States. She lives in Escondido, California, United States.

Haley Moore Parents

She is the daughter of Michele Moore, Tom Moore. Moore’s parents were college athletes. Her father was the long snapper on punts, field goals, and extra points for the Ohio State football team, and her mother played tennis for the Ohio State Buckeyes. After they got married Moore’s parents played a little golf at the executive courses and munis in San Diego. Moore’s grandfather was a big golfer and he introduced Moore and his brother Tyler to the game. Moore’s elder brother Tyler was born in 1996, he also attended the University of Arizona and played on the men’s team.

In 2020, Moore’s mother speaking about her daughter, she wrote in an open letter; “Now, my daughter is a full-fledged member of the LPGA Tour. She might not look like everyone else. But that doesn’t matter. She is kind; she is happy; she is a fierce competitor inside the ropes and the best friend you could have outside of them. She has game and heart, determination and mental toughness. She was a bullied child who grew up to be the strongest woman I know. She is my daughter. She is my world.”

Haley Moore Weight Loss

During the 2020 season, due to the tight traveling and playing schedule, Moore lost 35 pounds. Moore told golf.com; “I could just tell out there that I was getting tired and fatigued from traveling week after week, So I wanted to use the long offseason to really try to lose some weight and get my strength up.”
