Following his roles in Next Friday and Friday After Next, actor Mike Epps became well-known. In addition to his work in television series and films, Mike gained notoriety when, barely two years after divorcing Mechelle McCain, he wed Kyra Robinson, his second wife.

Mike Epps and his spouse, Kyra Robinson Epps, have been wed for more than a year.

On June 23, 2019, Mike and Kyra, his partner of a few years, exchanged vows at the famed Pelican Hil Resort in Newport Beach, California. The special guests included well-known celebrities including Doug E. Fresh, Tiny, T.I., and Snoop Dogg.

Mike’s bride donned a gown designed by her own company, Pedram Couture. Epps wore Christian Louboutin heels and a custom Tom Ford tuxedo.

Famed producer Kyra is the wife of Mike Epps. She is currently employed by the OWN network on the popular program Iyanla Fix My Life.

The Engagement of Mike and Kyra: How Did They First Meet?

In May 2018, Mike Epps and Kyra Robinson became engaged. On April 2, Kyra shared the exciting news on her Instagram.

According to AD sources, the couple got together at an airport towards the end of 2017. Before things started to get quite amorous between them, the two stayed close friends for a few months.

In December 2019, the celebrity couple revealed that they were expecting their first child. On her own Instagram profile, Kyra posted several pictures that showed off her noticeable baby bulge.

Mechelle McCain, Mike Epps’s ex-wife, is the mother of their two children.

On July 28, 2006, Mike Epps wed Mechelle McCain for the first time. According to sources, they got together for the first time while filming The Fighting Temptations in 2003. The pair decided to advance their relationship after dating for a few years.

Moriah Epps, born in 2005, and Maddie Epps, born in 2007, are the divorced couple’s two kids.

Citing an “irreconcilable difference,” Mike filed for divorce from Mechelle in January 2016. Following their September 2017 divorce, the court mandated that Epps give Mechelle $25,000 per month in spousal maintenance for six years. After that, Mike began seeing Kyra Robinson, his former girlfriend.
