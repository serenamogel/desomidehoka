Gabby Otchere Darko Believes John Mahama Is Preparing Grounds For His Comeback In 2024

 Posted on June 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  237 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The New Patriotic Party NPP leading member, Gabby Asare Otchere Darko has asserted that John Mahama claims to have won the election is an agenda to launch his comeback in 2024.

John Dramani Mahama who is the former president of the Republic has indicated that his party will not accept the results declared by the Electoral Commission as the Presidential results.

He noted that they won the election but the EC rigged it for the ruling NPP hence they won’t accept.

However, Gabby Otchere Darko believes that it’s an agenda to win sympathy for the former president’s brand to pave way for him to come back in 2024.

He made this known via his Facebook page noting that the minority leader in Parliament Haruna Iddrisu and Elvis Ankrah are supporting John Mahama to make a strong case to help him return as the flagbearer.

He wrote, “So, does any serious person in NDC actually believe that their presidential candidate did not lose the 2020 election? I doubt that. Or, are they all, including the ambitious lot like Haruna Iddrisu and Elvis Afriyie Ankrah, helping JM to build a “stronger” case to be returned as flagbearer for the 4th time in 2024? I hope they have considered all that!”
