Anthony Deion Branch is a former American football wide receiver who played for the New England Patriots, Seattle Seahawks, and Indianapolis Colts in the National Football League. He was born on July 18, 1979, in Albany, Georgia, USA (NFL). With the Patriots, Branch won Super Bowl XXXVIII and Super Bowl XXXIX, and was named Super Bowl MVP in the second. From 2002 through 2014, Branch was a professional athlete.
