Who Owns Patron? Patron Company History

 Posted on July 2, 2024  |  17 minutes  |  3478 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Welcome to our article on the fascinating history of Patron, one of the leading brands in the tequila industry. In this section, we will explore the ownership and evolution of Patron, uncovering its remarkable journey from a small tequila startup to a globally recognized brand.

Patron was founded by John Paul Dejoria and Martin Crowley in 1989. The brand emerged out of their shared vision to introduce a high-quality tequila to the United States market. Recognizing the lack of premium tequila options, Dejoria and Crowley set out to create their own brand, and thus, Patron was born.

Initially, Patron partnered with Casa 7 Leguas, one of the oldest Mexican distilleries, to produce its first batch of tequila. However, in 1989, Dejoria and Crowley purchased the brand rights, and production was later moved to a new distillery.

Under the leadership of CEO Ed Brown, Patron experienced tremendous growth and success. However, in January 2018, the brand took a new direction when it was sold to Bacardi, one of the world’s largest privately held spirits companies, for a staggering $5.1 billion.

Despite the change in ownership, Patron has continued to flourish and maintain its position as a premier tequila brand. Let’s delve deeper into the fascinating origins and success of Patron in the following sections.

The Origins of Patrón Tequila

Patrón Tequila, renowned for its premium quality and exquisite flavor, was founded in 1989 by John Paul Dejoria and Martin Crowley. The story behind the brand’s inception is one of serendipity and a shared passion for exceptional tequila.

It all began when Martin Crowley gifted John Paul Dejoria a hand-blown tequila bottle from Mexico. Recognizing the lack of high-quality tequila options in the US market, Dejoria and Crowley saw an opportunity to create their own brand of exceptional tequila.

In collaboration with the esteemed Mexican distillery Casa 7 Leguas, Patrón’s inaugural production consisted of 12,000 bottles. This initial release captured the attention of tequila enthusiasts and connoisseurs, sparking a buzz that would eventually pave the way for Patrón’s rise in popularity.

From its humble beginnings, Patrón Tequila has emerged as a standard-bearer for excellence in the industry. Let’s explore the brand’s journey, its meticulous production process, and the wide range of exceptional tequilas it offers.

Ownership and Management Changes

In 2000, Ed Brown, co-founder of Patrón and former Seagram executive, assumed the role of CEO at the company. With his leadership, Patrón experienced significant growth and expanded its presence in the tequila market.

However, in January 2018, a major change occurred when Patrón was acquired by Bacardi, one of the world’s largest privately held spirits companies. The acquisition was valued at $5.1 billion, solidifying Bacardi’s position in the tequila industry and giving them ownership of the beloved Patrón brand.

Despite the change in ownership, Patrón has remained true to its roots and continues to thrive under Bacardi’s stewardship. The brand’s commitment to quality and craftsmanship has not wavered, and it maintains its status as a premium tequila revered by connoisseurs around the world.

The Impact of New Ownership

  • Bacardi’s global distribution network has helped Patrón reach new markets and expand its customer base.
  • The acquisition has provided Patrón with additional resources and support to further enhance its production processes and maintain the exceptional quality of its tequilas.
  • Patrón has benefitted from Bacardi’s extensive industry knowledge and expertise, leveraging their insights to stay ahead of market trends and consumer preferences.

The seamless transition of ownership has allowed Patrón to continue its legacy of excellence and innovation. By combining the strengths of both Patrón and Bacardi, the brand remains at the forefront of the tequila industry, delivering exceptional products and experiences to tequila enthusiasts worldwide.

The Patrón Hacienda and Production Process

The Patrón Hacienda, located in Atotonilco El Alto, Mexico, is where the magic of Patrón tequila comes to life. Spanning across a sprawling estate, the hacienda serves as the heart of the brand’s production, blending traditional methods and modern techniques to create their signature tequila.

At the Patrón Hacienda, the production process begins with the careful cultivation of blue agave plants, which are grown on-site. These plants are meticulously nurtured until they reach their optimal maturity, ready to be transformed into tequila.

The agave hearts, also known as “corazon,” are then carefully harvested and prepared for the next steps in the production process. Each agave heart is cooked to perfection, unlocking the rich flavors within. Once cooked, the hearts are crushed to extract their sweet juices, which will become the base of the tequila.

Through a process of fermentation and distillation, the agave juice is transformed into the smooth, high-quality tequila that Patrón is renowned for. This process combines the expertise of skilled artisans with state-of-the-art equipment, ensuring a consistently exceptional product.

The Patrón Hacienda takes pride in preserving the tradition and heritage of tequila production, while also embracing innovation to enhance the quality and efficiency of their processes. The result is a tequila that captures the essence of agave and delivers a delightful tasting experience.

The Range of Patrón Tequilas

Patrón offers a wide range of tequila varieties, each with its own unique flavor profile. From smooth and elegant to bold and complex, there is a Patrón tequila to suit every palate.

Patrón Silver

Patrón Silver is a classic and versatile tequila, known for its smoothness and subtle citrus notes. It is perfect for sipping or mixing in cocktails.

Patrón Añejo

Patrón Añejo is aged for at least one year in oak barrels, resulting in a rich and complex flavor profile. With hints of vanilla, caramel, and oak, it is a true delight for tequila enthusiasts.

Patrón Reposado

Patrón Reposado is aged for a minimum of two months, which adds a touch of oak flavor and a smooth character to the tequila. It strikes a perfect balance between the fresh agave notes and the aging process.

Gran Patrón Burdeos

Gran Patrón Burdeos is an ultra-premium tequila that undergoes a unique aging process in Bordeaux wine barrels. This results in a full-bodied and velvety smooth tequila with rich flavors of ripe fruit and oak.

Flavored Tequilas

In addition to the classic varieties, Patrón also offers a selection of flavored tequilas that add a twist of excitement to your drinking experience.

Some of the popular flavored tequilas include:

  • Patrón XO Cafe
  • Patrón Citrónge
  • Patrón Mango

These flavored tequilas infuse the natural flavors of coffee, citrus, and mango into the smooth Patrón base, creating unique taste sensations.

Whether you prefer the pure agave flavor of Silver, the complexity of Añejo, the smoothness of Reposado, or a flavored tequila for added excitement, Patrón has a wide range of options to satisfy your tequila cravings.
