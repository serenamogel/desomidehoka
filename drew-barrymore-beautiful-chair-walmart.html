Drew Barrymore's Sold-Out Boucle Chair Just Restocked!

 Posted on May 31, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  403 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

If you've been keeping up with the latest interior design trends, you've probably noticed a major resurgence of boucle upholstery. The wooly fabric is a perfect way to add warmth and texture to your space, and you can often find it in the form of cozy couches and stylish accent chairs. However, you don't have to splurge to achieve the look in your own home; Drew Barrymore's under-$300 boucle accent chair just got restocked at Walmart in a brand-new color.

Beautiful Drew Chair by Drew Barrymore

Now 17% Off

Credit: Walmart

The Drew Chair, which initially launched in April of this year from Drew Barrymore's brand, Beautiful, sold out at Walmart within days of its release. Featuring a barrel-shaped silhouette and roomy seating wrapped in soft boucle fabric, it's shockingly budget-friendly at just $298 (compared to other boucle accent chairs that start at $600). The swiveling design makes it easy to get in and out of, and the streamlined silhouette is easy to style in any room in your home. Plus, it even comes with three plush accent pillows for extra cushioning.

In addition to re-stocking the original white Drew Chair, Beautiful also added an eye-catching sage green hue and a matching coffee table to its Walmart collection. Both items use the same classic boucle upholstery and streamlined silhouette as the original chair, and their rounded design makes them easy to style in tight corners without appearing cramped. Plus, the coffee table has a removable wooden top, making it easy to rest drinks and decor on top while providing hidden storage for blankets, board games, and more underneath.

No matter which furniture accent catches your eye, you'll want to add them to your cart quickly. Given the popularity of the original Beautiful boucle chair and the low price point, we wouldn't be surprised if it sold out fast again! Hurry to Walmart, where Barrymore's Beautiful line is exclusively sold, for these effortlessly stylish pieces in the brand-new colorway before they're gone.
