 Posted on April 7, 2024  |  8 minutes  |  1648 words  |  Martina Birk

Wow! What a wonderful gay-themed movie.

It's on Showtime this month, and I honestly didn't know what to expect.

The actors in this movie were incredible. Not only because they were so young, but because they were so convincing!

The story was excellent (although endings are always difficult for writers, this movie included).

The two leads were very handsome. Félix Lefebvre was such a cute twink, and although it took me a little bit to appreciate Benjamin Voisin's looks, by the end of the movie I was smitten!

I'm so proud of these two French actors, because they were amazing. Better than any American actors in their age group.

Unlike most gay-themed movies, this one had so much heart and emotion in it.

If you get a chance, please watch this movie. It's so worth your time.
